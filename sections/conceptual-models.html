ACE Pyramid
from CDC (2020)
- One of the earliest, paradigmatic models relating ACEs to later-life health and well-being - CDC/Kaiser Permanente ACE Study (1995-1997) - Surveyed 17,000 subjects regarding childhood experiences and current health status. - Found Negative lifelong impacts on well-being - ACE pyramid is conceptual framework for the study. - Near base of pyramid is disrupted neurodevelopment (neuroplasticity) - They posited that disrupted neurodevelopment cascades into later life, leading to negative outcomes
