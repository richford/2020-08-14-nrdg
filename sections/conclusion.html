Conclusions and open problems

Conclusions

  • Not all adversity experiences are equal
  • Mixed-adversity samples are inconsistent.
  • Must distinguish adversity types and control for co-occurence.

    • Threat

    • Alterations to
      • fronto-amygdala circuit
      • hippocampus
    • Adaptive aversive learning

    Deprivation

    • Alterations to
      • frontoparietal network
      • frontostriatal function
    • Early excessive pruning
  • Evidence for global accelerated development, but not confined to amygdala-mPFC.
